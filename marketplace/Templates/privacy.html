At CashstronomyClo, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, shipping/billing address.
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details, PayPal, or other payment methods.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, products viewed, and other analytics data.
  • Cookies & Tracking Data: To enhance your browsing experience offers.
2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for purposes including:

  • Processing and fulfilling your orders.
  • Sending order confirmations, shipping updates, and promotional emails.
  • Improving our website, services, and customer experience.
  • Preventing fraudulent activity and ensuring secure transactions.
3. Sharing Your Information

Your personal information is never sold to third parties. However, we may share it with trusted service providers who help us operate our business — such as payment processors, delivery companies, and marketing platforms. These partners are only given the information necessary to perform their tasks and are required to protect your data in accordance with privacy laws. We may also disclose information if required by law or to safeguard our rights.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Cookies help us remember your preferences, keep items in your cart, and analyze site performance. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly without them.

5. Data Protection & Security

Your security is our top priority. We use:

  • Encryption technology to protect payment details.
  • Secure servers to store personal data.
  • Access controls to prevent unauthorized entry.

While no system is completely foolproof, we take every possible measure to keep your data safe.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to request access to your personal information, correct any inaccuracies, or request that we delete your data. You can also opt out of receiving marketing communications by clicking the “unsubscribe” link in our emails or contacting us directly. We respect your choices and will honor your privacy preferences.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here, and the “Last Updated” date will be modified accordingly.
